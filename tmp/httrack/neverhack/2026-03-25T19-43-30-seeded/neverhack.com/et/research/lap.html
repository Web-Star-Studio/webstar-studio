LAP INNOVERY (Laboratorio Accreditato di Prova: Accredited Test Laboratory)

The LAP INNOVERY project provides for the creation of a LAP (Laboratorio Accreditato di Prova: Accredited Test Laboratory) where it is possible to carry out continuous technological assessment and testing of the security of electronic equipment and applications used for the delivery of critical services by the Subjects included in the National Cybersecurity Perimeter (PSNC: Perimetro Nazionale di Sicurezza Cibernetica), thus contributing to the construction of a network of technological testing laboratories to support the National Assessment and Certification Centre (CVCN: Centro di Valutazione e Certificazione Nazionale) established at the National Cybersecurity Agency.
