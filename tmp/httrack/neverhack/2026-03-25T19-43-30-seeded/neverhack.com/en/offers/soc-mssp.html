Methodology

How to get it?

Our methodology begins with establishing a centralized SOC for 24/7 monitoring, ensuring continuous coverage of your IT environment. We integrate existing cybersecurity tools or deploy advanced detection technologies like Extended Detection and Response (XDR) to enhance threat visibility. Automatic alert enrichment, coupled with real-time incident response, minimizes false positives and accelerates decision-making. Regular control and reporting provide transparency into your security posture, enabling proactive management and continuous improvement.

01

NEVERHACK SOCs

Our SOCs are centralized centers of excellence, leveraging local teams for 24/7 coverage. Each SOC benefits from centralized knowledge, delivering real-time incident response.

02

Extended Detection and Response (XDR)

If SIEM and XDR are deployed, we can integrate them into your infrastructure. If not, we can deploy an XDR agent for advanced threat detection and visibility.

03

Automatic Alert Enrichment

Alerts are enriched using IOC data and OSINT, helping to accelerate incident response times with reduced false positives.

04

Control and Reporting

Our services provide transparent reporting with visibility into your security posture, enabling faster decisions during security events.
