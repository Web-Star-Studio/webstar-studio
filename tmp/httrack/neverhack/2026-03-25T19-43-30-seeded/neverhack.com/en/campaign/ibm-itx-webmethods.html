IBM ITX + IBM webMethods

Data transformation and SaaS integration in a single powerful platform

Companies managing complex flows between systems, partners and applications know that data transformation and integration can become a brake on innovation.

The combination of IBM Sterling Transformation Extender (ITX) and IBM webMethods Hybrid Integration eliminates this limitation, creating a single agile, scalable and cloud-ready platform.

ITX ensures advanced data transformation, while webMethods introduces speed, flexibility and SaaS agility. The result is an end-to-end ecosystem where data, applications and processes collaborate seamlessly.

According to Forrester: up to 67% reduction in integration time and over 170% ROI in three years.


IBM ITX — The most powerful data transformation engine


  1. High-performance transformation engine for large volumes
  2. Native support for hundreds of formats, including EDI, SWIFT, SEPA, JSON, XML, COBOL
  3. Advanced mapping and configurable business rules
  4. Graphical modeling to reduce custom development
  5. Flexible deployment: on-premise, cloud, container/Kubernetes


IBM webMethods Hybrid Integration — SaaS agility and modern integration


  1. API Management & Gateway to design, secure and govern APIs
  2. Hybrid Integration Runtime to connect on-premise, cloud, microservices and legacy applications
  3. Event-driven Integration with Kafka, JMS, MQ and cloud hubs
  4. Managed File Transfer integrated into application workflows
  5. B2B & EDI Gateway for partner and supply chain management
  6. SaaS connectors (Salesforce, SAP, ServiceNow, Microsoft 365, etc.)


The NEVERHACK advantage

  1. 20+ years of enterprise experience

  3. Certified expertise in integration, cloud, AI and cybersecurity
  4. Robust processes and methodologies to ensure continuity and security
